# Product Overview

## Product Definition

Journey Buddi is a web and mobile application that combines AI-driven trip planning with real-time environmental intelligence to create adaptive, optimized travel itineraries for extended adventure and nature trips.

## Value Proposition

### For Travelers (B2C — Primary)

**"Your smartest travel companion — one that knows the destination better than any guidebook and adapts your trip in real time so you experience everything at its absolute best."**

Key value pillars:

1. **Save 20+ hours of planning**: Instead of weeks researching and organizing, have a structured conversation with an AI that knows the destination deeply and builds your perfect itinerary
2. **Experience optimal conditions**: See the glacier on the clear day, not the cloudy one. Watch dolphins when they're actually active. Hike when the weather is ideal.
3. **Never miss the hidden gems**: Get tips that only locals and experienced guides know — best viewpoints, timing tricks, preparation advice
4. **Stress-free adaptability**: When conditions change, get clear guidance on what to do instead, with step-by-step logistics for the swap
5. **Personalized to you**: Not generic top-10 lists, but an itinerary shaped by your pace, interests, fitness, and style

### For Local Businesses (B2B — Future)

In later phases, local activity providers, accommodation operators, and transport companies can gain visibility within Journey Buddi's recommendation engine. When Buddi suggests a kayak tour in Abel Tasman, the provider behind that suggestion benefits from targeted, high-intent exposure.

## Product Pillars

### 1. AI-Guided Planning
A conversational AI that builds your itinerary through structured dialogue — asking about your interests, presenting concrete options, and assembling a coherent trip plan.

### 2. Environmental Intelligence
Integration with weather, tide, wind, solar activity, and other environmental data sources to understand real-world conditions and their impact on activities.

### 3. Adaptive Itinerary Engine
A decision system that continuously evaluates upcoming activities against current and forecasted conditions, suggesting swaps when they materially improve the experience.

### 4. Expert Knowledge Layer
Deep, destination-specific knowledge about attractions, timing, logistics, hidden tips, stories, and practical guidance — delivered contextually when the user needs it.

### 5. Daily Companion
Morning briefings, packing lists, timing guidance, and proactive suggestions that make each day feel guided and optimized.

## How It Works — User Perspective

### Step 1: Tell Buddi About Yourself
A friendly onboarding conversation where Buddi learns your travel style through concrete choices:
- Adventure level (mild, moderate, extreme)
- Interests (wildlife, landscapes, water activities, culture, food, photography...)
- Physical fitness (easy walks, moderate hikes, strenuous treks)
- Pace preference (relaxed with downtime, balanced, packed adventure)
- Budget range for activities
- Travel dates and duration flexibility

### Step 2: Build Your Dream Trip
Buddi presents destination-specific options organized by category:
- "Would you like to include glacier experiences? In NZ, you can do Fox Glacier or Franz Josef — here's how they differ..."
- "Dolphin swimming is available in Kaikoura, Akaroa, and Bay of Islands. Given your route, Kaikoura fits best and has the highest encounter rates."
- Buddi handles the routing, grouping, and scheduling logic internally

### Step 3: Review & Refine
Buddi presents the complete itinerary:
- Day-by-day breakdown with activities, transport, and accommodation zones
- Total trip duration recommendation
- Transport mode recommendations per segment with reasoning
- The user can adjust, swap, add, or remove — Buddi re-optimizes in response

### Step 4: Live Companion Mode
Once the trip begins:
- Morning notification with daily briefing and condition assessment
- Activity-specific guidance as each activity approaches
- Proactive swap suggestions when conditions warrant
- On-demand chat with Buddi for questions, tips, or changes

## Competitive Landscape

See [COMPETITIVE-ANALYSIS.md](./COMPETITIVE-ANALYSIS.md) for detailed breakdown.

**TL;DR**: No existing product combines AI-driven conversational planning, real-time environmental intelligence, and adaptive itinerary management. TripAdvisor and GetYourGuide are transactional (book this thing). Google Travel is aggregation. Wanderlog is a better spreadsheet. None of them adapt to conditions or provide expert-level guidance.

## Revenue Model (Future)

Revenue is intentionally deferred to focus on building an exceptional product and user base. Potential models for later:

1. **Freemium**: Basic planning free, live companion features as premium subscription
2. **B2B Marketplace**: Local providers pay for visibility/featured placement in recommendations
3. **Premium Destinations**: Advanced destination packs with deeper knowledge bases
4. **Affiliate**: Commission on bookings made through Buddi's recommendations

For now, the app is free with no authentication complexity beyond basic persistence.

## Key Metrics (Future)

- **Itineraries Created**: Volume of trip plans generated
- **Active Trip Companions**: Users currently on a trip with Buddi active
- **Swap Acceptance Rate**: How often users accept Buddi's suggested itinerary changes
- **Condition Accuracy**: How well our environmental intelligence predicted actual conditions
- **NPS / User Satisfaction**: Post-trip satisfaction scores
- **Return Usage**: Users who plan a second trip with Buddi
